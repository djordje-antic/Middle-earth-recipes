Frogmorton Flapjack

Back to main


Description

A good bite for the sweet-tooth from the 'real' Shire.

Ingredients

  • 125g Porridge oats (quick oats)
  • 125g Rolled oats
  • 150g butter
  • 75g golden syrup
  • 75g caster sugar (superfine)
  • 1 tsp. ginger (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the butter, syrup and sugar in a saucepan until all melted together.
  2. Then mix in the oats.
  3. Place in a baking tray and cook for about 30 minutes at 175 degrees
  4. Then slice, and leave to cool.
