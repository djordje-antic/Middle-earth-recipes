Balin's Spiced Beef

Back to Main


Description

Something from the finer days, when he entertained his cousins in style.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 lb shoulder or round roast beef
  • Dry red wine
  • One or two sliced white onions
  • 1/2 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp. each of cinnamon and allspice
  • 1/2 t. cloves
  • 1 1/2 t. salt
  • 1 t. pepper

Steps

  1. In a big bowl put meat and cover it with ingredients from above and let it marinate in the fridge for 12 hrs. or more.
  2. Drain and reserve marinade.
  3. Preheat oven to 275 degrees.
  4. Put meat in a roasting pan
  5. Heat half the marinade to boiling and pour it over along with about a cup of boiling water.
  6. Cover and simmer in the oven for about 3 hours.
  7. If you like, you can add carrot chunks, sliced turnip and potato an hour before its done.
  8. You can thicken the sauce like gravy, or have it au jus.
  9. Serve with roasted veggies, or noodles, or rice. Good hot or cold.
